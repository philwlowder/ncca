Durham Academy Master Trek 25
          Saturday, Dec. 5, 2009

LOCATION:
            Durham Academy Middle School (Taylor Hall)
          3116 Academy Road  Durham , NC 27707-2699

Sections:
 A,B,C and D etc.   8 players  each. Top 8 are placed into A section next 8 in B and so on.   Lower sections will have flexible sizes as needed.  This event is aimed to give players an opportunity to play serious rated chess against those of similar ability.  Organizer reserves right to adjust section sizes if wide rating variance can be avoided.

Prizes:
 1st Place Trophy.  Sections  with more than 8 players 2nd place trophy’s.  Place ribbons available to all. 

Schedule:
First round in all sections is 8:30 a.m.  Rounds are paired as soon as last game in given section finishes.  If you are last game you can request a rest period before your next game begins.

Time Controls:
Sections A, B, C  G/60  -- D and below G30 .  3 rounds

Requirements:
Open to full time students of any age, or by invitation.  USCF membership is required.
Non students maybe included to fill out sections if odd number..
