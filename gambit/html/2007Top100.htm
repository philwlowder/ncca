North Carolina Players Scattered Among Top 100 Lists

The following players are listed in the 2007 year-end Top 100 lists by the U.S. Chess Federation. They are comprised of current USCF members, who have North Carolina as their state affiliation.

Hometowns are listed for most players. But some are missing. Feel free to drop the Webmaster a line to correct anything – or offer additional information. Check out all of the lists at the USCF.
