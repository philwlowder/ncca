N.C. State Scholastic Champions
1973 - Present

The N.C. State Scholastic began in 1973 -- and focused mainly on high school players. In the first few years, the tournament was entirely open, with all players competing in one section. As the tournament grew, grade sections were added. In some years, there was a K-6 and K-5 section. In this table, those winners have been combined.
