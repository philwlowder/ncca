Open Section At N.C. Open Offers A Few Extras

Winning the Top Section at the North Carolina Open, Nov. 2-4 in Greensboro, comes with a few extra advantages:

  • All N.C. players who tie for first in the Open Section are State Champions. See our list of past winners.

  • The top two N.C. players, based on tiebreaks, get automatic invitations to the upcoming North Carolina Invitational.

  • For the past two years, the Internet Chess Club has conducted an online Champion of State Champions event – with the winner advancing to the U.S. Championships. Last year, FM Miles Ardaman advanced to the semis of the ICC event.
