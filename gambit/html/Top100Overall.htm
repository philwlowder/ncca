Top 50 Active Players in North Carolina

The following is a list of the Top 50 overall chess players in North Carolina as judged by the December 2007 USCF ratings. Players listed meet the following criteria:

1.  Are current USCF members (Has your membership lapsed? It's easy to correct.)
2.  Have North Carolina has their state affiliation (This affects some out-of-state college students.)
3.  Have had some rating action taken during 2007. (Did you remember to play a rated game in 2007?)

Hometowns are listed with some players. But I don't know everyone. So feel free to drop me line to add some information - or correct a mistake.
