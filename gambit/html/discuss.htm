We are pleased to Announce...

The New NC Chess Discussion Forum

 

The NC Discussion Board had become unstable recently due to corruption of the database.  It became impossible to post a message.  This likely occurred when the site was sold to another party.  We have been hard at work to not only fix the problem, but to prevent any reoccurrence in the future.  This involved starting from scratch.

Now for the good news:  We have a new and improved Discussion Forum up and running.  While we were unable to move the original database, the old data has not been lost.  You may still view it, but I have locked future access to the old forums. 

There are many advantages to the new system.  It is now hosted on our own server, so there are no more annoying  advertisements.  The bulletin board software is up to date, more secure, and has more features.  Perhaps best of all, we should be able to back up the database regularly to avoid any future  catastrophic failure.

It will be necessary to register on the new bulletin board.  We apologize for the inconvenience this will cause.  Your participation is a valued part of the website.

I'm looking for ONE HUNDRED to sign up in the next month.  Please help us reach our goal, and get those groovy names before they are all snatched up!
