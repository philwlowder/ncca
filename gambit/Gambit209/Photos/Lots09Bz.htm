Land of the Sky XXII

Speed Chess Tournament

 

Seventeen competitors gathered Saturday morning with clock cleaning on their minds.  The resulting chess melee attracted a crowd of eager spectators, and reportedly left a few slap-happy.

I have elected to present the majority of these photos under the theory that "a picture is worth a thousand words."  A second and equally valid theory is that my fingers may fall off if I continue typing!
