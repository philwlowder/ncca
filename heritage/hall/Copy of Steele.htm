N.C. Chess Hall of Fame

 

 

NM David Steele  (1943--       )

                                                                

About me:  I was born Dec 19, 1943 in Columbia, SC.  US Department of Agriculture moved m father around to various places until we finally settled in Raleigh, where I attended Myrtle Underwood Elementary School, Josephus Daniels Junior High School, Broughton High School, and NC State University.

I learned to play chess at the old Vass-Barden Chess Club in Raleigh when I was 13 years old.  I recall with fondness my mentors there:

Dan Diano

Dr. Al Jenkins

Dr. Stuart Noblin

Dr. Norman Hornstein

O.N. Rich

Paul Newton

Ed Blanchard

...and many others.
