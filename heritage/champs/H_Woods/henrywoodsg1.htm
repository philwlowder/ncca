C51: Evans Gambit: Declined and Accepted without 5...Ba5 (Editor's NOTE) Henry Woods was the first official NC Chess Champion. He earned the title for 1935, tied in 1936, won again in 1938, and tied in 1946. However, records are missing for many years in between, so King Henry may have won more than four times. Our research is ongoing, and we welcome any information you may have on this our first NC Chess Champion. (Tom Hales, Editor NC Gambit)
