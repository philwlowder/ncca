Henry Woods

Our First Known NC Chess Champion

1935, 1936, 1938, 1946

 

BIOGRAPHY:   

Henry Woods was from Black Mountain, North Carolina, and is reportedly buried there.  Send any information you have to chess@triad.rr.com.   Anything is helpful, including town of residence, dates, and of course photos.

NOTE:  Since there are many gaps in our records from this period, Woods was probably champion in other years.
