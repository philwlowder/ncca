2011 North Carolina Open

  • $12,500 guaranteed payout with $2,000 for 1st place!!
  • Cash prizes 10 places deep in each section!
  • Open section is FIDE rated
  • 100 USCF Grand Prix points
  • Extremely low entry fee for a weekend tournament!
  • Additional Monday blitz tournament.

When:

Labor Day Weekend: Sept. 2 -4 (3-Day), or Sept. 3-4 (2-Day); Blitz on Sept. 5th

Where:

The Blake Hotel - Uptown Charlotte, 555 S. McDowell St., Charlotte, NC 28204.
Reservations: 1-888-664-6835. Mention North Carolina Chess Association for a special rate of $79 plus tax. Must call by August 15, 2011 to be assured of the special rate. Rate available from Aug. 31 through Sept. 6. This is a first-class hotel with a huge playing space. FREE PARKING for players.
