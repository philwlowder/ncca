I live in Raleigh but will travel throughout the triangle. If you need any other info just let me know.

Peter is currently ranked 14th overall in the state of North Carolina, with a rating of 2107 USCF and 2094 FIDE. Both individual and group instruction in the Charlotte, NC area are offered.

"I have coached two South Florida school teams to FIRST PLACE (Under 1200 Section) at K-12 Nationals. Along with more than 10 years experience coaching, I am a certified TD, an 1800 rated player, and an English teacher at Cannon School in Concord."

"I only take a handful of private players at a time so you can expect an individualized teaching plan along with reasonable rates. I will travel to your home if desired. Individual lessons best for players USCF rated 100-1200. I also run a Chess Camp for two weeks at Cannon School for beginners."
