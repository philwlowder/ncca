CMSCA Presents

Third Annual CMSCA

Scholastic Class Championship

(sections determined by rating levels, or “class”)

Saturday, May 22, 2010

(Online Registration – click here)

 

Where:            Olympic High School, 4301 Sandy Porter Rd., Charlotte, NC 28273

 

Format:          Swiss System (no one is eliminated). No last round byes.*

6 Sections:     Open, Under 1400, Under 1000, Under 750, Under 500, Under 250 (we will use the June 2010 USCF Rating Supplement and/or most current rating)

 

All sections open to scholastic (preK-12th) players.  Collegiate players 22 and younger are invited to play in the Open section only!

 

Entry Fee:      $25 pre-registration – on site $30.00 (8:30 – 9:00 AM)

 

Memberships: USCF membership required. Memberships available at tournament or online at www.uschess.org   Players without documented USCF membership must pay the dues to be allowed to play.  

 

Round Times and Time Control:

Open, U1400 (3 rounds, G/75): 9:30am, 12:45pm, 3:30pm

U1000, U750, U500, U250 (4 rounds; G/45): 9:30am, 11:30am, 1:30pm, 3:30pm;

 

Prizes (subject to change based on entries):

Open: 1st place $50, 2nd place $25, Best U1600 $10

U 1400: 1st place $35, 2nd place $20, Best U1200 $10

U1000, U750, U500, U250: Trophies to top 3, Medals to any tied for last trophy place.

 

Registration:  May ratings supplement at USCF will be used (www.uschess.org); unrated can enter any section. If you register after 9:00 AM you cannot play the first round and will receive a half-point bye.  Bring chess equipment, notation sheets & clocks. Food and chess equipment will be available for purchase.  For further information contact Tim Champion at tchampion@jcsu.edu or Lori Holcombe at LDH4duke@bellsouth.net. If you pre-register but cannot play, notify Tim before the first round for a refund.

 

Byes:  One 1/2pt. bye can be requested for any round.  Registering after 9 a.m. uses this bye for round one.  Extra rounds missed will be a zero point bye.  We must be notified in advance of byes or the player will be forfeited, withdrawn from the tournament, and not allowed to play in later rounds.  Byes will be posted for pre-registrations on the web (www.cmsca.org) and on the wall charts after each round; correction requests are the responsibility of the player.
